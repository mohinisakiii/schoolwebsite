ACTIVITY CENTER

Nurturing Young Minds, the Umanandan Way
Umanandan - a name that resonates with wisdom, respect, and leadership, inspired by Lord Ganesha himself. At Umanandan Activity Center, we strive to imbibe these qualities in children during their formative years, laying the foundation for a bright and successful future.
At Umanandan, we specialize in working with children aged 3 to 7—those formative years that shape their personalities and skills. Through thoughtfully designed fun games and activities, we blend learning with enjoyment.

Our unique approach is well-researched and proven to help children thrive. They not only develop good habits and hobbies but also acquire critical life skills such as:
• Critical thinking and problem-solving
• Creative and verbal skills
• Shlok recitation and storytelling
• Breathing techniques for mindfulness
Outdoor activities are another highlight! Each session begins with warm-up exercises, followed by free play under the guidance of our expert and compassionate instructors. Your child’s safety and well-being are our top priorities.
To accommodate school schedules, our sessions are held in the evenings from 5:00 PM to 6:30 PM, offering a perfect balance between play, learning, and relaxation.
Umanandan is more than just an activity center; it’s a place where your child’s potential is nurtured and their foundation strengthened. Invest in their future today, and watch them shine tomorrow!
